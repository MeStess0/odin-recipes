A grilled, steamed, or boiled sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun.

Ingredients

  • Hot Dogs (Franks/Wieners)
  • Hot Dog Buns
  • Butter or Oil
  • Souce/s
  1. Heat the Pan: Place the frying pan on the stove over medium heat. Add about 1 teaspoon of butter or oil.
  2. Sear the Franks: Once the butter is melted or the oil is shimmering, carefully place the hot dogs in the pan.
  3. Turn and Brown: Cook for 5 to 8 minutes, using tongs to turn the hot dogs frequently. You want a nice brown sear and for the hot dogs to be heated completely through.
  4. Remove: Take the cooked hot dogs out of the pan and set them on a plate lined with a paper towel to absorb any excess grease.
  5. Assemble: Place one hot dog securely into the middle of each warm bun.
  6. Top: Squeeze a zigzag of mustard over the hot dog. Add ketchup and/or relish as desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
